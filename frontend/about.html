About Orenda

A calm, structured space for reflective writing.

What is Orenda?

Orenda is an AI journaling experience designed to help you notice emotions, name patterns, and choose gentle next steps. It’s meant to feel supportive—never rushed.

How it Works

You write a journal entry, select your mood, and then Orenda generates a structured reflection.

Privacy

Your entry is processed securely to generate a reflection. Avoid entering highly sensitive personal information.

Mental Health Disclaimer

Orenda is not a substitute for professional care. If you need urgent help, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional.

Tech Stack

FastAPI for the backend, Gemini AI for reflections, and a calm frontend built with HTML/CSS/Vanilla JavaScript.

Architecture Overview

Designed to connect easily with FastAPI endpoints:

  • journal.html collects mood + entry (front-end form)
  • Future endpoint receives the payload and returns reflection cards
  • history.html can render saved entries and summaries
